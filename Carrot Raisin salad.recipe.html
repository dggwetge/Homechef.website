Carrot Raisin Salad

Ingredients

  • 3 cups shredded carrots

  • 1/2 cup raisins

  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts or pecans(optional)

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the shredded carrots, raisins, and chopped nuts (if using)

  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, honey or maple syrup, lemon juice, and salt until well combined

  3. Pour the dressing over the carrot mixture and toss until everything is evenly coated

  4. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary

  5. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow the flavors to meld together

  6. Serve cold as a refreshing side dish or light snack

    7. Enjoy!
