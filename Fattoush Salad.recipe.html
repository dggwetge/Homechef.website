Fattoush Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes, sliced

  • 1 cucumber,diced

  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

  • 1 cup chopped fresh parsley

  • 1 cup chopped fresh mint

  • 2 pita breads, toasted and broken into pieces

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, parsley, and mint

  2. Add the toasted pita bread pieces to the bowl

  3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the salad

  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. Toss everything together until well combined

    6. Enjoy!
