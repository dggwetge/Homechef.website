Pesto Zucchini Noddle

Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchinis, spiralized into noodles

  • 1/2 cup basil leaves

  • 1/4 cup goated Parmesan chesse/

  • 2 cloves garlic

  • 1/4 cup olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine basil leaves, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and garlic

  2. Pluse, until finely chopped.

  3. With the motor running, slowly pour in olive oil until smooth

  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste

  5. Toss Zucchini noddles with pesto until evenly coated

  6. Serve immediately, optionally garnished with extra Parmesan cheese
