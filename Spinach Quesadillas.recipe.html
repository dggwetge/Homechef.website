Spinach Quesadillas

Ingredients

  • 4 large flour tortillas

  • 2 cups of fresh spinach leaves

  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as Monterey Jack or cheddar)

  • 1/2 cup diced onions

  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes

  • 1 teaspoon olive oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Optional: salsa, sour cream, or guacamole for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add diced onions and cook until softened, about 2-3 minutes

  2. Add fresh spinach leaves to the skillet and cook until wilted, about 1-2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste

  3. Remove the spinach and onion mixture from the skillet and set aside

  4. Place one flour tortilla in the skillet. Sprinkle half of the shredded cheese evenly over the tortilla

  5. Spoon half of the spinach and onion mixture evenly over the cheese

  6. Sprinkle diced tomatoes over the spinach mixture

  7. Top with the remaining shredded cheese and place another flour tortilla on top to form a quesadilla

  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortillas are golden brown and the cheese is melted

  9. Remove from the skillet and let cool for a minute before slicing into wedges

  10. Serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole if desired

    11. Enjoy!
