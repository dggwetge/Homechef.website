Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (5 oz each) of tuna, drained

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise

  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

  • 1/4 cup chopped celery

  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion

  • 1/4 cup chopped pickles or relish (optional)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Squeeze of lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, flake the drained tuna using a fork

  2. Add mayonnaise and Dijon mustard to the tuna, and mix until well combined

  3. Stir in chopped celery, red onion, and pickles or relish if using

  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add a squeeze of lemon juice for extra flavor if desired

  5. Mix all ingredients together until evenly distributed

  6. Serve immediately as a sandwich filling, on crackers, over salad greens, or as desired

    7. Enjoy!
