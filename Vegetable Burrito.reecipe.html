Vegane Burrito

  • 1 large flour tortilla

  • 1/2 cup cooked rice (white or brown)

  • 1/2 cup black beans (cooked or canned, drained and rinsed)

  • 1/4 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)

  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes

  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers (any color)

  • 1/4 cup diced onion

  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or Mexican blend)

  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (optional)

  • Salsa, guacamole, or sour cream for serving (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the tortilla in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make it pliable.

  2. Spread the cooked rice evenly over the tortilla, leaving about an inch border around the edges.

  3. Layer the black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, bell peppers, onion, shredded cheese, and chopped cilantro (if using) on top of the rice.

  4. Fold the sides of the tortilla in towards the center, then roll it up tightly from the bottom to enclose the filling.

  5. Optional: Heat the burrito in the skillet or microwave for a minute or two to melt the cheese and warm everything through.

  6. Serve with salsa, guacamole, or sour cream on the side for dipping or drizzling, if desired.
