Vegetabe Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as potatoes, carrots, peas, cauliflower)

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 2 tablespoonginger, minced

  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk

  • 2 tablespoons curry powder

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat

  2. Add chopped onion, garlic, and ginger, saut√© until fragrant

  3. Stir in curry powder and cook for 1 minute

  4. Add mixed vegetables and cook until slightly softened

  5. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for about 15-20 minutes until vegetables are tender

  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste

  7. Serve hot over rice or with naan bread

    8. Enjoy!
