Vegetabe Paella

Ingredients

  • 1 cup paella rice

  • 2 cups vegetable broth

  • 1 onion, diced

  • 2 cloves garlic, miced

  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced

  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 1 cup green pas

  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika

  • 1 pinch saffron threads(optional)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a paella pan or large skillet over medium heat

  2. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until softened

  3. Add bell peppers and cook until slightly tenderAdd bell peppers and cook until slightly tender

  4. Stir in rice, smoked paprika, and saffron threads (if using)

  5. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a simmer

  6. Cook for about 15-20 minutes until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed

  7. Stir in cherry tomatoes and green peas, cook for another 5 minutes

  8. Season with salt and pepper

  9. Serve hot, granished with fresh parsley if desired

    10. Enjoy!
