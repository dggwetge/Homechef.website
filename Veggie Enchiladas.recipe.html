Veggie Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, zucchini, corn)

  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed

  • 1 cup enchilada sauce

  • 1 cup shredded cheese (such as cheddar or Monterey Jack)

  • Optional toppings: diced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream, salsa

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a baking dish with cooking spray or oil

  2. In a skillet, sauté the mixed vegetables until they are tender-crisp, about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the black beans and cook for another 2 minutes

  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave for a few seconds to make them pliable

  4. Spoon a portion of the vegetable and bean mixture onto each tortilla, then roll them up tightly and place them seam-side down in the prepared baking dish

  5. Pour the enchilada sauce evenly over the rolled tortillas

  6. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the top of the enchiladas

  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly

  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving

  9. Garnish with diced avocado, chopped cilantro, sour cream, and salsa if desired

    10. Enjoy!
